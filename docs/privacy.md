## Information We Collect

### 1. Chat Messages
When you interact with the AI assistant, your messages are sent to Groq's API for processing. Messages are used solely to generate responses and are not stored permanently by the app.

### 2. Analytics Data
We use Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used:
- App opens and session duration
- Features used
- Device type and operating system version

This data is collected anonymously and does not identify you personally.

### 3. Crash Reports
Firebase Crashlytics automatically collects crash data when the app encounters errors, including:
- Stack traces
- Device model and OS version
- App state at time of crash

This helps us identify and fix bugs.

### 4. Device Identifiers
The app may collect:
- Advertising ID (for analytics purposes)
- Installation identifier (for crash reporting)

### 5. Audio Data (Voice Input)
If you use voice input, audio is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

## Data Retention

- **Chat messages:** Not stored after your session ends
- **Analytics data:** Retained for 14 months (Firebase default)
- **Crash reports:** Retained for 90 days

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data:
- All data transmitted uses encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- No sensitive personal data is stored on our servers

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Request deletion:** Contact us to request deletion of any data associated with your device
- **Opt-out of analytics:** Disable usage data sharing in Android Settings > Privacy
